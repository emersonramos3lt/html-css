It's a great source of fibre

Apples contain a lot of fibre, which is very beneficial for improving the body's metabolism. Do not throw away the apple peel when you consume an apple. Studies have shown that eating apple peels can promote good health. This is because apple peels contain a high amount of fibre, which aids in the process of detoxification and keeps your liver free of toxins.

It has low-fat content

Green apples are low in fat and have a high-water content, which helps to keep the blood flowing. Improved blood circulation lowers the risk of heart disease and stroke. Additionally, green apples contain a high amount of Vitamin K, which prevents blood clots from forming.
